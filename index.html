How it works

From behavioral signal to embedded guidance

01

You already have what we need

Response behavior, care adherence patterns, and channel engagement history. Clear signals, already in your systems. Your outreach strategy is not reading them.

02

Patterns become posture

We read those signals across time to classify motivational posture: the durable orientation toward care that persists across interactions, and the situational shifts that occur under stress or at high-stakes moments.

03

Posture becomes action

Channel, tone, timing, and escalation guidance lands directly in the workflow your team is already using. No translation step. No new tool.
